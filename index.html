About the Festival

Empowering the next generation of filmmakers

Our Mission

The Virginia Student Film Festival is dedicated to celebrating and promoting the work of talented student filmmakers across Virginia. We provide a platform for emerging artists to showcase their creativity, connect with industry professionals, and gain recognition for their craft.

Our Vision

To be Virginia's premier platform for student filmmakers to share their stories and launch their careers.

Our Values

Creativity, diversity, excellence, and community in filmmaking.

Our Community

The festival is made possible by a dedicated team of students and industry professionals who believe in the power of student storytelling. Together, we create an environment where creativity thrives and future filmmakers can flourish.

Screenings & Awards

Submission Guidelines

  • Film must be created by a currently enrolled student at a Virginia educational institution
  • Runtime must not exceed 15 minutes (including credits)
  • Film must have been completed within the last 2 years
  • Accepted formats: MP4, MOV, or AVI (HD 1080p preferred)
  • All genres welcome: narrative, documentary, experimental, animation, etc.
  • Films must have proper rights clearance for all music and content

What You'll Need

  • Film file (HD quality)
  • Film synopsis (max 250 words)
  • Director's statement
  • Film stills (3-5 high-res images)
  • Proof of student status
  • Credits list
